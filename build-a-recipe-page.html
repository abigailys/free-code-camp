Enoki Minestrina

Warm hearty Chinese-Italian fusion soup (for 2) brought to you by Eva and Abigail!

Ingredients

  • 1 shallot, sliced
  • 1 packet enoki mushrooms, bottom removed
  • 100g small pasta
  • 500ml mushroom stock

Instructions

  1. Heat up oil in a wide pot
  2. Lightly fry shallots until fragrant
  3. Add soup stock and bring to a boil
  4. Add pasta and boil according to the instructions on the packet
  5. 3 minutes before completion, add the enoki mushrooms
